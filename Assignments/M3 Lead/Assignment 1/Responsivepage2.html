The City

Coimbatore is the second largest city in Tamil Nadu and it is located in the south Indian state of Tamil Nadu.

What is the old name of Coimbatore?

Kongunadu and also known as Kovai

What is the first language of Coimbatore?

Tamil

When was Coimbatore founded?

In 1804,Coimbatore was established as the capital of the newly formed coimbatore district.
